Meet Stefan

Your Local Wisconsin Tattoo Artist

Originally from La Crosse, Wisconsin, and now based in Viroqua, Stefan brings 5 years of dedicated tattoo artistry to the Driftless region. With a passion for creating meaningful, custom artwork that tells your story, Stefan specializes in bringing your vision to life with precision and artistry.

Whether you want to visit Stefan's studio in Viroqua or prefer the convenience of having him come to you, Stefan offers flexible scheduling to make your tattoo experience as comfortable as possible. Each piece is crafted with attention to detail, using only the highest quality inks and maintaining the strictest safety standards.
